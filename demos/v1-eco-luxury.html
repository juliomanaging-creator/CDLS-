How It Works

MCMC Route
Intelligence

847,000 California haul records. 10 years. The path of least resistance — calibrated for human reality.

I

MCMC Regression — Noise Removal

Markov Chain Monte Carlo sampling across 847K haul records eliminates seasonal noise, fuel spikes, and economic volatility — surfacing true route cost patterns with 94.7% confidence.

II

A/B Testing — Stabilization

Controlled re-introduction of real-world variance via A/B testing (p=.003) validates the model against live dispatch data, ensuring predictions hold under real California conditions.

III

Human Behavior Calibration

Routes adjusted for documented driver behavior — fatigue windows, familiar corridors, preferred stops — achieving 91.3% fit with routes humans actually complete successfully.
