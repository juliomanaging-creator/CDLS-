DCAP — Down Payment Grants

California's primary active EV incentive for 2026. The Driving Clean Assistance Program (DCAP) provides upfront down-payment grants paid directly to the dealer — no repayment required. Two pathways depending on your situation: scrap-and-replace (higher amount) or financing assistance. Income must be at or below 300% of the federal poverty level (~$93,600 for a family of four). Vehicles must be priced at $45,000 or less. You must apply before purchasing — not retroactive.

Home EV Charger Tax Credit
Expires June 30, 2026
$1,000

The Alternative Fuel Vehicle Refueling Property Credit (Section 30C) covers 30% of the cost of purchasing and installing a qualifying Level 2 home EV charger — up to $1,000 for homeowners. Equipment must be installed and placed in service before July 1, 2026. Eligible census tracts only — our team verifies your address at no charge. Claimed on your federal tax return (Form 8911).

Federal · OBBBA · 2025–2028
Vehicle Loan Interest Deduction
$10,000/yr

New under the One Big Beautiful Bill Act (July 2025): buyers can deduct up to $10,000 per year in interest paid on a qualifying new vehicle loan. Applies 2025–2028. Vehicle must have final assembly in the United States. Income limit: MAGI up to $100,000 single / $200,000 joint for the full deduction. Above-the-line deduction — no itemizing required. Ask us which Mitsubishi models qualify under the US-assembly requirement.

Programs That Are No Longer Available in 2026
Federal IRA EV Tax Credit
Expired: September 30, 2025

The $7,500 new EV credit and $4,000 used EV credit were eliminated by the One Big Beautiful Bill Act signed July 4, 2025. Not available for vehicles acquired after September 30, 2025.

California CVRP Rebate
Closed: November 8, 2023

The Clean Vehicle Rebate Project stopped accepting applications in November 2023. All funding was reserved for applicants on the waitlist at that time. No new applications accepted.

CAV / HOV Lane Decals
Expired: October 1, 2025

All California Clean Air Vehicle decals expired October 1, 2025. Solo HOV lane access for EV owners is no longer part of the active incentive picture as of 2026.
